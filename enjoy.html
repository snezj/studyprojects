ENJOY:

Curated experiences in food, art, and culture. Join us on select nights and weekends in Downtown Norfolk, Virginia.

INTRODUCING ENJOY: A space dedicated to the joy of creative exploration. We’re inviting handpicked chefs, artists, and entrepreneurs to share their creative passions with the community. Whether it’s to try something new or to share a favorite concept with a new audience, each experience will support a charity of its choosing. ENJOY: is purpose-built to host a broad range of creative ideas, and to simplify the challenges of bringing those ideas to life.
1,500 sqft of flexible space The entire storefront can adapt to support different concepts, including a retractable garage door and seating for up to 49 people.
